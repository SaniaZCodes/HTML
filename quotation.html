GLOBAL BOOK REVIEWS

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Review Summary:
This book is a masterpiece. As the author says:

"When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."

Analysis:
It is often compared to the style of the UN charter on human potential.

The Plot:
The story follows a boy seeking his Personal Legend. This theme is explored deeply in the book: The Journey of the Soul.
